About Us

Prem Dance Studio was established on June 5, 2024, by founder Rohit Tiwari, with co-founders Shivanshu Mishra and Saurabh Yadav. Our studio offers a range of classes including Zumba, Aerobics, Yoga, and Meditation. We provide a creative space for all dance enthusiasts to explore and express their passion.

Our trainers, Prem Chopra and Shivam Kumar, are dedicated to bringing out the best in every student. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced dancer, you'll find a welcoming and energetic environment at Prem Dance Studio.
